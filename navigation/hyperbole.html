Hyperbole

Exaggerations that are so dramatic that no one would believe it is true.

Videos

Examples

Hyperbole Cafe

Welcome to our restaurant
Where every thing's gigantic.
A hundred waiters hold one dish.
Our kitchen can get frantic.
Our noodles stretch a mile
The bread is longer than a train.
It's sure to make you smile.
We pile our peas up mountain high.
One cookie hides the moon.
We pour our iced tea into boats
We hope you'll visit soon.

Timeout

All by myself.
Alone in a room.
So lonely, it's been 10,000 years,
So lonely no one remembers me at all,
So lonely my whole world is just this room.
Oh wait!
My 5 minutes are up.
Goodbye, I'm going out to play!

Quiz

Can you find the hyperbole? Hover over to see if a line is a hyperbole or not. If the hover is green, it is a hyperbole. If the hover is red, it is not a hyperbole.
